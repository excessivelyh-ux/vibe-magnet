
## SMM Panel Landing Page — TikTok & Instagram Followers

A bold, colorful single-page website that showcases follower packages for TikTok and Instagram, with WhatsApp as the main contact/order method.

### Pages & Sections

**Single Landing Page with these sections:**

1. **Hero Section** — Eye-catching gradient background with bold headline like "Boost Your Social Media Presence", subtitle about TikTok & Instagram followers, and a prominent "Order Now" WhatsApp CTA button

2. **Services Section** — Two cards (TikTok Followers & Instagram Followers) with platform icons, brief descriptions, and "Order via WhatsApp" buttons

3. **Pricing Packages** — 3-4 tiered packages per platform (e.g. 100, 500, 1K, 5K followers) displayed as pricing cards with:
   - Follower count
   - Price
   - Delivery time estimate
   - WhatsApp order button for each

4. **How It Works** — 3-step visual flow: Choose Package → Contact via WhatsApp → Get Followers

5. **Why Choose Us** — Trust badges/features: Fast Delivery, 24/7 Support, Secure Payment, Real Followers

6. **FAQ Section** — Accordion with common questions about delivery, safety, refunds

7. **Footer** — WhatsApp link, social media links, copyright

### Design
- **Bold & colorful** with vibrant gradients (pink → purple → blue)
- Platform-branded accent colors (TikTok teal/pink, Instagram gradient)
- Rounded cards with subtle shadows and hover animations
- Responsive design for mobile-first (since target audience is social media users)
- WhatsApp floating button in bottom-right corner
